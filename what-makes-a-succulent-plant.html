What makes a Succulent Plant?

How SucculentCity.org classifies plants as 'succulent'.

by Joe Bowman

Everyone is an Expert

If you grow a plant and it should flourish under your care then you know a little about what it takes to understand the plant. Even if you do not claim to have 'plant knowledge' you have already proven to yourself (and your plant) that you understand something more. Perhaps you're thinking, "but I didn't even water it!" And, while this particular non-action may feel like 'doing nothing' in fact, you have had the sense (intentional or otherwise) not to water. Starting with a simple realization you are taking the first steps on the road to becoming an expert.
