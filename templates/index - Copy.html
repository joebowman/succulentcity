Propagating Succulent Plants

The SucculentCity Guide to Propagating Succulent Plants.

One of the most popular aspects of succulent plants is their adaptability and in harsh environments, being able to cling to life even when the main plant is damaged or destroyed. The survival strategies of succulent plants can be used to grow your collection, year after year.

By propagating your succulent plants, you can have the stock to create beautiful succulent wreaths and planters. You'll have so many new succulent plants you can give them away to guests or bring them to parties as hostess gifts.

Leaf Cuttings

Many succulent plants can be propagated from a single leaf.

The leaves of succulent plants will naturally form roots and if they get water and a little light, you'll see a little plant growing. Learn which types of succulent plants are easier to propagate by leaf cutting and ways you can help the new plantlet start out.
